About


This website is for those who are taking or going to take the civil service exam in the Phillipines. This website aims to help people who are taking the exam by providing a list of free reviewers available online. We made you an electronic mock exam from a selected sources in the internet. By doing so, we hope that the digitalized mock exams or quizes save you the hassle of writing and taking the mock exams by paper. Our goal for this website is to be more helpfull to all the Filipino citizen taking the civil service exam.

Disclaimer: We do not own the exams, quizes, questions, and answers listed in the source page. More so, we want to supplement those webistes so that their audience can take their mock exam conveniently.
