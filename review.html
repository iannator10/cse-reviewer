Civil Service Exam Reviewer and Coverage



The Philippine Civil Service Examination covers the following subjects written in either English or Filipino:


  • English and Filipino
  • Vocabulary
  • Grammar and correct usage
  • Paragraph organization
  • Reading comprehension
  • Analogy
  • Logic
  • Numerical reasoning
  • Clerical Operations
  • General Information about – Philippine Constitution; Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards for Public Officials and Employees (R. A. 6713); peace and human rights issues and concepts; and environment management and protection.


    • The professional level is harder than the sub-professional level.

    The former is a 170-item test with an allotted time of 3 hours and 10 minutes while the latter is comprised of 165-multiple choice questions and you have to finish it in 2 hours and 40 minutes.

    The Sub-professional level also has similar coverage but doesn’t cover Analogy and Logic but has Clerical Operations questions.
